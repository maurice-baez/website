About Me

From Healthcare to Software

I completed Rithm School's full stack web development program, which is centered around JavaScript and Python.

Prior to this endeavor, I worked as a Sonographer (ultrasound) for 10 years in small and large hospital settings. Working in healthcare has instilled in me excellent communication skills, problem-solving abilities, extreme attention to detail, and a diligent work ethic. While the work was incredibly rewarding, I felt that the scope of my impact was limited. Working in the industry, I saw firsthand how technology was able to impact at a larger scale as well as reduce redundant and repetitive work. As a result, I decided to make a career pivot into software development in order to create products that delight and unlock increased productivity for end users.

My goal is to ultimately blend my experience working in healthcare with the knowledge and skills I now possess to build relevant, scalable, and impactful products and platforms.

Other features added include; liking and unliking posts and functionality to edit the users profile and change the existing password.
